This layout uses float to line up each <figure> with its neighbor. The grid is nice and tidy because all of the images have the same aspect ratio; even if they differed in size, the proportion is the same. This is one of the simplest layouts, especially considering there are no margins (gaps) or captions to account for.

The downside to this approach is that all images must be edited to the same aspect ratio for this approach to look right.
